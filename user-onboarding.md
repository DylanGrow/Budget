## Getting Started

### Step 1: Install the App

#### On Mobile (iOS/Android)
1. Open this website in your browser (Safari, Chrome, or Firefox)
2. Look for the "Add to Home Screen" or "Install" prompt
3. Tap **Add** or **Install**
4. The app icon will appear on your home screen
5. Open it like any other app - it works offline!

#### On Desktop (Windows/Mac/Linux)
1. Open this website in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox
2. Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar
3. Click **Install**
4. The app will open in its own window
5. Access it from your applications menu

### Step 2: Add Your First Transaction

1. On the home screen, you'll see a **Quick Add** form
2. Enter the amount (e.g., 25.99)
3. Select a category (e.g., "Food & Dining")
4. Optionally add merchant name
5. Click **Add**

That's it! Your transaction is saved locally on your device.

### Step 3: Set Up Your Budget (Optional)

1. Tap the **Categories** tab at the bottom
2. Choose a category (e.g., "Food & Dining")
3. Tap **Set Budget**
4. Enter your monthly budget amount
5. Set an alert threshold (e.g., 80% to get notified)
6. Save

You'll now see your spending progress for that category!

## Understanding Offline-First

### What Does "Offline-First" Mean?

This app is designed to work **without an internet connection**. Here's what that means:

✅ **You Can:**
- Add, edit, and delete transactions anytime
- View all your data instantly
- Create budgets and track spending
- Generate reports and charts
- Use all features without internet

❌ **You Cannot:**
- Sync data across devices automatically (use encrypted backups instead)
- Access your data from another device without importing a backup
- Recover data if you lose your device without a backup

### Why This Matters

**Traditional apps** store your data on their servers. If their servers go down, you can't access your data. If they shut down, you lose everything.

**This app** stores everything on YOUR device. You're in complete control. The app works forever, even if the website goes offline.

## Backup Strategy (IMPORTANT!)

Since your data only exists on your device, **creating regular backups is crucial**.

### When to Create Backups

- ✅ **Weekly**: If you use the app daily
- ✅ **Monthly**: If you use it occasionally
- ✅ **Before**: Switching devices or clearing browser data
- ✅ **After**: Major data entry sessions

### How to Create a Backup

1. Tap the **Settings** tab (⚙️)
2. Select **Backup & Export**
3. Tap **Create Encrypted Backup**
4. Enter a strong passphrase (at least 12 characters)
5. Confirm your passphrase
6. Tap **Create Backup**
7. Save the downloaded file somewhere safe:
   - Cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, iCloud)
   - External hard drive
   - USB drive
   - Email it to yourself

### Choosing a Strong Passphrase

Your backup is encrypted with military-grade encryption (AES-256), but it's only as strong as your passphrase.

**Good Passphrases:**
- ✅ "MyBudget2026!SecureBackup"
- ✅ "correct-horse-battery-staple"
- ✅ "ILove2SaveMoney#2026"

**Bad Passphrases:**
- ❌ "password"
- ❌ "123456"
- ❌ Your name or birthday

**Pro Tip**: Write down your passphrase and store it securely. If you forget it, your backup cannot be recovered.

## Restoring from Backup

### Moving to a New Device

1. On your new device, open the app
2. Tap **Settings** → **Backup & Export**
3. Tap **Choose File** under "Restore from Backup"
4. Select your backup file
5. Choose restore strategy:
   - **Merge**: Combines with existing data (keeps both)
   - **Replace**: Deletes existing data (fresh start)
6. Enter your passphrase
7. Tap **Restore Backup**

Your data is now on the new device!

### If You Lose Your Device

If you have a backup:
1. Install the app on a new device
2. Follow the restore steps above

If you don't have a backup:
- Unfortunately, your data cannot be recovered
- This is why regular backups are essential!

## Privacy & Security

### What Data is Stored?

**On Your Device:**
- Transaction amounts, dates, categories
- Merchant names and notes
- Receipt images
- Budget settings
- App preferences

**NOT Stored Anywhere:**
- Your name or identity
- Bank account information
- Credit card numbers
- Social security number
- Any personal identifiers

### How is Data Protected?

1. **Local Storage**: Data never leaves your device
2. **Encrypted Backups**: AES-256 encryption with your passphrase
3. **No Tracking**: No analytics, no cookies, no third-party scripts
4. **Secure Connection**: HTTPS encryption when accessing the app
5. **Content Security Policy**: Prevents malicious code injection

### Can Anyone Access My Data?

**No.** Your data is stored in your browser's IndexedDB, which is:
- Isolated per website (other sites can't access it)
- Protected by browser security
- Only accessible when you're using the app
- Encrypted in backups with your passphrase

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Load
- **Check internet connection** (needed for first load only)
- **Clear browser cache** and reload
- **Try a different browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Update your browser** to the latest version

### Transactions Not Saving
- **Check storage quota**: Settings → Storage
- **Clear old data**: Delete old transactions or receipts
- **Create a backup first**: Before clearing data
- **Check browser permissions**: Allow storage for this site

### Backup Won't Restore
- **Verify passphrase**: Must match exactly (case-sensitive)
- **Check file integrity**: Ensure file wasn't corrupted
- **Try different browser**: Some browsers handle files differently
- **Contact support**: If file is definitely correct

### Storage Almost Full
1. **Delete old receipts**: They take up the most space
2. **Export to CSV**: Keep records outside the app
3. **Create backup**: Before deleting anything
4. **Delete old transactions**: Keep only recent data

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is my data synced to the cloud?
**A:** No. All data stays on your device. Use encrypted backups to transfer between devices.

### Q: What happens if I clear my browser data?
**A:** You'll lose all transactions unless you have a backup. Always backup before clearing browser data!

### Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?
**A:** Yes, but you need to manually export from one device and import to another using encrypted backups.

### Q: Is there a mobile app?
**A:** This IS a mobile app! Install it to your home screen for the full app experience.

### Q: How much does it cost?
**A:** It's completely free. No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden fees.

### Q: Can I export my data?
**A:** Yes! Export to encrypted JSON (for backups) or CSV (for spreadsheets).

### Q: What if I forget my backup passphrase?
**A:** Unfortunately, encrypted backups cannot be recovered without the passphrase. This is by design for security.

### Q: Does it work on older phones?
**A:** It works on any modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) from the last 2-3 years.

### Q: Can I import data from other apps?
**A:** You can import CSV files with the correct format (Date, Amount, Category, etc.).

### Q: Is my data really private?
**A:** Yes. We don't have servers, accounts, or any way to access your data. It's 100% local to your device.
